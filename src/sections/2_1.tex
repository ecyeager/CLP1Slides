\begin{frame}[t]{Slope of Secant and Tangent Line}
\begin{block}{Slope}
Recall, the slope of a line is given by any of the following:
\begin{align*}
\frac{\mbox{rise}}{\mbox{run}}&&\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}&&
\frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}\end{align*}
\end{block}
\end{frame}
